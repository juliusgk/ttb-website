Why Germany?

Booming

ECONOMY

Home to many of the greatest products and brands in the world. Innovation, scientific and academic excellence and a thriving workforce are characteristics for Germany's economy.

Florishing

WORK-LIFE BALANCE

Germany placed 4th in the Global Work-Life Balance Index due to its intensive employee protection laws and benefits. A big contrast to the USA is a minimum vacation days of 29 days. Alongside hard and focused work, German compaies believe in offering its employees an environment for them to florish, as well as recover and enjoy a good life.

Top

QUALITY OF LIFE

Germany is defined by its beautiful and diverse scenery, nature and wildlife, besides its buzzing cities and communities. As a multicultural community in the heart of Europa, everyone can find their favorite things to do and enjoy a life that exceeds their expectations.

Germany combines investment with academic excellence. Top researchers around the world are researching and developing in german Start-ups and university. This creates an atmosphere of possibilities and innovative thinking and attracts the best talents every year as Germany ranks 2nd in the world in international researchers and scientists.

Yes, you need a visa. However, Germany is very welcoming of U.S. citizens and offers a so-called Blue card. Particularly in jobs with a labor shortage in Germany, visa's are fairly easily attainable.

Germany has a very strong labor protection system. This means that you are entitled to a minimum of 29 days of vacation, as well as a minimum wage of 12 Euro per hour. The healthcare system is universal and free for all citizens and residents.

While it is not a requirement to learn German, it is highly recommended. Many companies in Germany operate in English, but knowing the language will help you integrate better into society and make your experience more enjoyable.
